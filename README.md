# Neo-Slow  
**Archive of Latent Futures**

---

## Concept

Neo-Slow is a platform for unrealized ideas.

It documents what is not yet built, not yet written, not yet possible —  
but already present.

Instead of waiting for ideas to become visible through history,  
Neo-Slow **archives the present**.

---

## Why

We often encounter important ideas too late.

Leonardo da Vinci's notebooks revealed concepts centuries after they were conceived.  
They show us not only what was realized, but what remained possible.

Neo-Slow shifts this condition:

From retrospective discovery  
to real-time preservation.

Unrealized ideas are not failures.  
They are **latent futures**.

---

## What Neo-Slow Does

- **Archives** unrealized ideas across disciplines  
- **Connects** them through themes, contributors, and relations  
- **Preserves** intellectual seeds before they disappear  

Neo-Slow is not a storage system.  
It is a living structure of thought.

---

## Structure

The platform is organized into:

- `/ideas` — Archive of unrealized projects  
- `/contributors` — People behind the ideas  
- `/themes` — Conceptual groupings and clusters  
- `/journal` — Essays, reflections, and context  
- `/map` — Visual relationships between ideas  
- `/about` — Platform logic and philosophy  

---

## Philosophy

Neo-Slow operates on different principles:

- No urgency  
- No optimization pressure  
- No algorithmic extraction  

Ideas do not need to be executed to exist.  
They can remain in a state of potential.

Neo-Slow creates space for that state.

---

## Tech

- Next.js (App Router)  
- Custom CSS (no Tailwind)  
- Modular component architecture  

---

## Status

Neo-Slow is an evolving platform.

This repository contains the initial structure and interface.  
Content will grow over time.

---

## Position

Neo-Slow does not document failure.  
It documents **timing**.
